About
Us

Our mission is to create beautiful, distinctive designs that enrich lives and communities. We aim to be timeless, approachable, and promote well-being. Our brand embodies exceptional design, ethical practices, and outstanding service.

At The Blueprint, we transform spaces into art. Our talented team crafts unique designs that reflect our clients' personalities, lifestyles, and values. From interiors to furniture, lighting, architecture, and landscape design, we approach each project with meticulous attention and creative fervor. As a sustainable and ethical company, we prioritize human connection and nature. Our designs are both beautiful and environmentally conscious, leaving a positive social impact. We don't chase trends but create enduring, artistic spaces. With offices in LA, Denver, and NYC, we offer comprehensive services nationwide. Christian Tyler

Christian, the principal designer, creative director, and founder of The Blueprint, draws inspiration from the profound human connection to physical environments and the imperative for beauty and reverence towards space. With an unwavering dedication, Christian crafts spaces that foster wellbeing and elevate the tangible realm. Having honed his craft in New York City alongside esteemed interior designers, Christian has forged a bespoke design methodology for The Blueprint, imbuing each project with value and fulfillment, while championing distinct, sustainable, and ethical creative practices and products. Guiding The Blueprint team, Christian epitomizes the pursuit of positive impacts on individuals and communities, embracing specialized design practices and unveiling unique aesthetics that redefine the boundaries of transformative design.
christian@theblueprint.design

Taylor Burkhardt

Taylor, Senior Interior Designer, has extensive design industry experience that spans high-end residential, commercial, and fashion design. She launched her career as a professional model and creative director in the vibrant heart of New York City. Driven by an unwavering passion for creating beauty, Taylor pursued interior design studies on both the east and west coasts, refining her discerning eye for detail and infusing The Blueprint design team with a sophisticated flair. With expertise in space planning, furniture layouts, material and finish selection, and lighting curation, Taylor fosters deep connections with clients, weaving their aspirations into personalized and timeless spaces while incorporating The Blueprint’s unique mission to elevate every project’s importance and create lasting influence.
